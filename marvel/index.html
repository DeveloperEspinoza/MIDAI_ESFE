Diamond Select Reveals New Taskmaster
Figure for Marvel Studios' 'Black Widow'

Phase four of the MCU kicks off in theaters on November 6.

Considering that Black Widow is a master assassin and spy, what foe could possibly foil
her? When Marvel Studios' Black Widow opens in theaters, we're going to find out --- it's
Taskmaster.

Ahead of the movie's release in November, Diamond Select has revealed the upcoming
Taskmaster figure that is sure to be a perfect addition to any ever-growing Marvel and
Black Window collection.

The figure, which was designed by Eamon O'Donoghue and sculpted by Gentle Giant
Studios, is approximately 7-inchs tall and has 16 points of articulation.Additionally,
Taskmaster comes with multiple accesories, including a shield, sword, bow, arrows, and
alternate hands.The 173rd release in the Marvel Select Line, Taskmaster comes packaged
on display-ready Select action figure packaging.
